about cth

Club Tengo Hambre is a roving supper club that explores the food, wine, spirits and craft beer of Baja California and Mexico City.

From the renowned street side stands of DF—as Mexico City is known—to Baja California’s cuisine, the exploding wine scene in the Valle de Guadalupe, Club Tengo Hambre gives curated excursions of Mexico’s best culinary offerings.
